❓ FAQs

What is the difference between IQ, OQ, and PQ?
IQ confirms equipment is installed correctly, OQ verifies it operates as intended, and PQ demonstrates it consistently produces conforming product under actual conditions.
How long does a process validation take?
Typically 3–6 months depending on the complexity of the process, number of products, and availability of equipment and resources.
Is CSV required for all computer systems?
Only GxP-critical computerized systems require CSV. A system inventory and impact assessment determines which systems fall in scope.
